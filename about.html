Who We Are

QLD Group is the parent company of a network of transportation companies, managing and operating several subsidiaries to provide reliable, integrated transport and logistics solutions.

Our Success Story & Partners
Since its establishment, QLD AB has consistently expanded its range of services to meet the diverse and growing needs of clients across both large and small sectors. The company has built a solid reputation in the Swedish market, partnering with renowned names such as DHL, BRING, DSV, POSTNORD, UPS, TNT, Fedex, MenigoGLC (Martin & Servera), among others.

QLD AB History

Founded

QLD AB was founded in Sweden on November 11, 2021, with a clear vision to become a leading partner in sustainable and reliable transport solutions for both businesses and individuals.

Our Vision

To be the trusted and sustainable partner in transport solutions, supporting companies of all sizes and our local community.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality transport services that meet the needs of all our clients, committed to the highest standards of safety and environmental sustainability while employing individuals of all abilities.

Our Values

Safety. Responsibility. Inclusivity. Excellence. Customer Service. Environmental Sustainability.

Our Strengths

  • Safety: Secure and reliable services with professional teams and well-equipped vehicles.
  • Specialization & Flexibility: Tailored solutions for large companies, small businesses, and individuals.
  • Sustainability: Commitment to environmental preservation and minimizing ecological footprint.
  • Inclusive Employment: Job opportunities for everyone, including individuals with disabilities and autism.
